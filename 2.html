The US photographer Susan Meiselas first began shooting women who took their clothes off for a living in 1972, when she was in her mid-20s.
Travelling around New England, she’d encountered the contry fairs that toured rural parts of the northeastern US; many had a ‘girl show’ tent, where women danced in striptease acts. Meiselas was fascinated. Over the course of three summers,
